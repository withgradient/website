Gradient – Streamline Your Order-to-Cash Process

Gradient is a B2B SaaS platform that helps companies accelerate and automate their order-to-cash workflows.

We help finance and sales teams improve cash flow and reduce manual work by automating the collection of key billing documents (like purchase orders), sending follow-up reminders, syncing information back into CRMs and ERPs, and flagging inconsistencies in orders and invoices.

What Gradient Does

  • Automated Order Collection: Gradient collects purchase orders and billing information directly from customers, using intelligent email workflows and real-time tracking.
  • Invoice & Subscription Sync: We integrate with your invoicing systems (e.g., Stripe, Netsuite) to monitor outstanding invoices and update statuses in your CRM.
  • CRM Data Enrichment: Gradient can push billing details and payment status back into your CRM (like Salesforce or Hubspot), keeping your sales team in sync.
  • Dunning Automation: Send smart payment reminders and escalation emails based on real-time invoice and subscription data.

Why Gradient Requests Access to Your Google Account

Gradient uses Google OAuth to authenticate users securely and may request access to:

  • Your basic profile and email address: to identify your account and associate your usage with your organization.
  • Your Gmail account (optional): if enabled, Gradient can send and track automated billing-related emails on your behalf.

You are always in control of what access you grant, and we only request the minimum required to provide our services.

Transparency & Trust

  • Gradient is hosted in the EU with enterprise-grade security practices.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Google account settings.
